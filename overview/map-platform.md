# MAP Platform

## What this is

MAP (Mapping Articulated Pathways) is California's statewide CPL infrastructure. Launched in 2017 as the Military Articulation Platform, expanded in 2020 to cover all CPL types, and integrated into the California Community Colleges Chancellor's Office in 2024. Available at no cost to all 119 credit and noncredit campuses. The MAP team at Riverside Community College District (RCCD) handles day-to-day operations and statewide scaling on behalf of the Chancellor's Office (CO) and in close collaboration with CO leadership in Academic Affairs and Workforce and Economic Development (WED) divisions. The initiative is situated organizationally in the Academic Affairs division.

Public surface: <https://map.rccd.edu>.
