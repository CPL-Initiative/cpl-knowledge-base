# Contributing

Thanks for considering a contribution to the CPL Knowledge Base.

## Scope

This repo is a curated public mirror of internal MAP team / CCCCO knowledge. The authoritative source is the private CPLBrain vault — most edits originate there and flow into this repo through the curation process documented in [CURATION.md](CURATION.md).

## What we welcome from external contributors

- **Issues** for factual corrections, broken links, outdated figures, or missing context
- **Issues** suggesting new content categories or methodology playbooks worth promoting
- **Pull requests** for typo fixes, formatting improvements, broken-link repairs

## What we do not accept via PR

- Substantive content additions — those are routed through the MAP team and the private vault first, then published here
- College-specific commentary or rankings
- Pre-release figures or unannounced funding commitments
