# Vision 2030 — A Roadmap for California Community Colleges (July 2025 edition)

> **Live data supersedes static counts.** Current student counts, equity outcomes, and goal-to-date progress are on the [CPL Project Dashboard](https://cpl-initiative.github.io/cpl-project-tracker/) and the [MAP CPL Insights Dashboard](https://cpldashboardcccco.azurewebsites.net/insights/dashboard). This file preserves the structure, goals, strategic directions, and CPL-specific references from the July 2025 edition.

## Summary

The overarching strategic plan for the California Community Colleges system, originally presented to the Board of Governors in July 2023, refreshed in July 2025 to incorporate two years of progress, emerging research, AI/technology advances, and the shifting landscape of higher education. Authored under Chancellor Sonya Christian's leadership with Board President Hildegarde B. Aguinaldo.

## Three goals

1. **Equity in Access** — Proactively take college to Californians wherever they are.
2. **Equity in Success** — Ensure all students complete credentials and achieve outcomes.
3. **Equity in Support** — Provide comprehensive support systems for student success.

## Three strategic directions

1. **Equitable Baccalaureate Attainment** — Transfer pathways and Baccalaureate Degree Programs (BDP).
2. **Equitable Workforce and Economic Development** — CPL, apprenticeships, industry partnerships.
3. **Generative AI and the Future of Learning** — HUMANS-centered AI principles.

## Key framing context

- Vision 2030 is **iterative, not prescriptive** — designed to support college districts in aligning local plans.
- Governor's target: 70% of working-age Californians hold a postsecondary degree or certificate by 2030.
- Statewide adult population context: a large share of working-age Californians remain without a postsecondary credential.

For current statewide student counts, FY economic-output figures, and BOG-tracked equity outcomes, see the dashboards.

## Key sections

### Section II — Taking care of students, communities, planet

- **Students:** dual enrollment (ninth-grade strategy), veteran support (MAP / Veteran Sprint), Rising Scholars (justice-involved), foster youth, working adults without credentials.
- **Communities:** rural community access, Community Resilience Centers (disaster response).
- **Planet:** climate action, microgrids, green-economy pathways.

### Section III — Goals and outcomes

Detailed metrics and targets for equity in access, success, and support. (Current values: dashboards.)

### Section IV — Strategic directions

- **Direction 1:** Transfer reform, BDP expansion.
- **Direction 2:** CPL, workforce development, employer partnerships — where the CPL Initiative and MAP platform are directly referenced.
- **Direction 3:** AI integration with HUMANS-centered principles (data privacy, human oversight, bias protection).

### Section V — Spheres of engagement

People, Systems, Policy, Resources. Includes Program Pathways Mapper, Common Cloud Data Platform, ERP, and microgrids.

## CPL-specific references in Vision 2030

- CPL is identified as a Vision 2030 Demonstration Project.
- The MAP Initiative and Veteran Sprint are explicitly highlighted in Section II under student veterans.
- The CPL Workplan is one of the Vision 2030 workplans (see [CPL Workplan](../policy-and-funding/cpl-workplan-2025.md)).
- **Action 1a** (Scale CPL) and **Action 5** (flexible scheduling and CPL for working learners) are the CPL-relevant action commitments.

## Governance

- President of the Board of Governors: Hildegarde B. Aguinaldo.
- 19 members representing students, faculty, trustees, public, classified employees, and the Lieutenant Governor.
