## Quick Summary

Platform for sharing success stories about Credit for Prior Learning (CPL) at California community colleges. The page invites students, faculty, counselors, and administrators to contribute narratives about how CPL has helped learners earn college credit and accelerate degree completion.

## Content Overview

**Mission**: "Your CPL Story Matters" — recognizing the valuable skills and knowledge students bring from military service, workplace training, certifications, and other experiences.

**Target Audience**:
- Students with prior learning experience
- Military veterans
- Working adults
- Faculty and counselors
- College administrators

**Key Value Proposition**: CPL helps students "earn college credit and accelerate their path to a degree or career advancement."

## Why This Matters

- **Community-driven resource**: Documents real impact of CPL across California's community college system
- **Narrative-building**: Success stories help marketing, outreach, and institutional advocacy for CPL expansion
- **Multi-stakeholder engagement**: Contributions from students, faculty, and admin strengthen program visibility
- **Evidence base**: Real-world examples support CPL implementation initiatives and policy advocacy
