## Quick Summary

Sample Word document providing survey question templates for assessing Credit for Prior Learning programs. Designed to help colleges gather feedback and data on CPL effectiveness, student experience, and program outcomes.

## Content Overview

**Document Type**: Survey research template (.docx format)

**Purpose**: Reference template for colleges developing CPL program assessment surveys covering student perceptions, outcomes, and program efficacy.

**Expected Coverage Areas** (typical CPL survey topics):
- Student awareness and understanding of CPL
- Accessibility and ease of process
- Quality of prior learning evaluation
- Credit award satisfaction
- Impact on degree completion timeline
- Faculty and counselor support quality
- Barriers and recommendations
- Demographic and cohort analysis

**Status**: Sample/template for institutional customization

## Why This Matters

- **Standardized assessment**: Provides common questions enabling cross-college benchmarking
- **Data collection**: Enables quantitative and qualitative feedback on CPL programs
- **Evidence-building**: Supports institutional reporting, policy advocacy, and improvement cycles
- **Stakeholder feedback**: Captures input from students, faculty, and staff on program effectiveness
- **Continuous improvement**: Data informs program refinements and scaling decisions
