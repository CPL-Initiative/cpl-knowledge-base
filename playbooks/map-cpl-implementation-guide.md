## Quick Summary

Comprehensive implementation guide designed to support all campus professionals involved in Credit for Prior Learning programs. Provides clear guidance, tools, and strategies for leveraging the MAP Platform to build, scale, and institutionalize CPL across California community colleges.

## Content Overview

**Target Audience**:
- Faculty
- CPL leads / coordinators
- Counselors
- Veterans Resource Center staff
- Admissions & Records evaluators

**Core Focus**: Implementing CPL "from the ground up" — organizational frameworks, process design, and institutional integration strategies.

**Framework**: Leveraging the MAP (Mapping Articulated Pathways) Initiative for "statewide credit recommendations to help veterans and working adults earn college credit for their prior learning experiences."

## Why This Matters

- **Comprehensive playbook**: Single resource for end-to-end CPL implementation
- **Cross-functional alignment**: Guidance for all campus stakeholder groups
- **Scalability focus**: Designed to grow CPL from pilot to institution-wide program
- **Evidence-based approach**: Based on MAP Platform tools and statewide best practices
- **Veteran and working-adult focus**: Targets high-impact populations
