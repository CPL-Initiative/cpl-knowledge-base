## Quick Summary

Comprehensive resource hub for California Community College counselors implementing and supporting CPL initiatives. Provides policy documents, administrative procedures, and guidance materials for helping students access credit for prior learning.

## Content Overview

**Key Policy & Governance Resources**:
- **BP/AP 4235 – Credit for Prior Learning**: Recommended district policy and administrative procedures
- **Title 5 §55050 – CPL Summary**: State regulation overview
- **CCCCO CPL Resources**: Statewide guidance and standards

**Support Focus**: Materials covering policy documents, Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), and transfer guides.

**Mission**: Support counselors in helping students access CPL opportunities under Title 5 §55050 for veterans and working adults.

## Why This Matters

- **Counselor enablement**: Equips front-line advisors with tools, policies, and regulatory guidance
- **Consistent implementation**: Aligns institutional practices with Title 5 state requirements
- **Transfer pathway clarity**: SOP and transfer guides ensure students understand CPL credit application
- **Policy documentation**: Reduces confusion and supports institutional governance
