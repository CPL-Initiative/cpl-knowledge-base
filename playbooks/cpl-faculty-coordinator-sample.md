## Quick Summary

Sample Word document providing a template for the CPL Faculty Coordinator position. This template defines the role, responsibilities, and qualifications for a faculty-level coordinator leading CPL implementation at a college.

## Content Overview

**Document Type**: HR/Position template (.docx format)

**Purpose**: Serves as a reference template for colleges developing their own CPL Faculty Coordinator job description to support institutional CPL programs.

**Expected Sections** (typical job description structure):
- Position title and reporting line
- Primary responsibilities and functions
- Required qualifications and experience
- Preferred qualifications
- Compensation and benefits reference
- FTES/workload allocation

**Status**: Sample/template for adaptation to individual college contexts

## Why This Matters

- **Standardized position definition**: Provides common language for faculty leadership roles in CPL
- **Scalability tool**: Enables colleges to quickly develop position descriptions based on proven model
- **Faculty engagement**: Formalizes faculty involvement in CPL institutional leadership
- **Institutional alignment**: Supports hiring, performance evaluation, and role clarity
