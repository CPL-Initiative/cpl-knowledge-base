# ESS 25-82 — Credit for Prior Learning Funding Memo (Dec 9, 2025)

> Issued under **AB 123** (Chaptered, July 2025) — [official chaptered version of the bill](https://map.rccd.edu/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/07292025-AB-123-Chaptered-Version.pdf).

## Summary
Official Chancellor's Office memorandum (ESS 25-82) from Vice Chancellor James E. Todd and Senior Advisor Samuel Lee directing $50,000 CPL implementation grants to all California Community Colleges under AB 123. Establishes the three systemwide priority outcomes, allowable uses, and reporting requirements.

## Key Details

### Funding
- **$50,000** per institution (credit colleges and noncredit campuses)
- Certification survey due **January 15, 2026** from Chief Instructional Officers
- Funds distributed **Spring 2026**, must be fully expended by **June 30, 2028**

### Three Priority Outcomes
1. **Awarding CPL Through JSTs:** Upload JSTs into MAP for at least the number of enrolled veterans reported to MIS for 2024-25; award and transcribe Basic Training credit and additional JST-based credits aligned with student programs
2. **Implementing Statewide Credit Recommendations:** Adopt or adapt applicable statewide recommendations from ASCCC Pathways to Credit posted on MAP
3. **Proactively Offering CPL:** Establish or strengthen local procedures to identify and screen students eligible for CPL; document CPL opportunities offered and credit transcribed in MAP

### Allowable Uses
- Processes to identify and notify CPL-eligible students during exploration, admissions, onboarding, and ed planning
- Coordination, student engagement, and counseling to expand CPL
- Procedures to award credit for industry certifications, military service, workplace training, foreign credentials
- Academic senate-driven policies honoring statewide credit recommendations

### Outcomes Reporting
- Tracked primarily through MAP platform plus MIS reporting
- Public metrics: military and non-military students served, CPL units available, CPL units transcribed
- Internal MAP metrics: Basic Training credit types, locally articulated units, CPL Review Request processing, disaggregated credit data

## Strategic Notes
- This memo is the operational implementation of AB 123's $15M one-time funding
- The three priority outcomes directly align with the Veteran Sprint goals and CPL Workplan dashboard tracking
- College of Sequoias was the only campus to decline the funding (per CPL Initiative Report)
