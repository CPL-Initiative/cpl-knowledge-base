## Quick Summary

Combined notification documents and compliance information for working with Veterans Affairs (VA) benefits and Credit for Prior Learning. Essential for ensuring CPL processes align with VA regulations and communication requirements.

## Why This Matters

- **VA Compliance**: Ensures CPL policies and processes comply with VA benefit regulations
- **Veteran Communication**: Required notifications and disclosures to veterans using VA benefits
- **Benefit Protection**: Ensures CPL awards don't negatively impact veterans' VA benefit eligibility or payments
- **Regulatory Alignment**: Critical for institutions serving significant veteran populations
